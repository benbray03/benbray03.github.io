Benjamin Bray

Statistics and Environmental Studies student studying marine ecosystems, fisheries, and coastal data science.

My work focuses on using statistical modeling, time series analysis, and geospatial tools to better understand marine ecosystems, fisheries, and coastal environments. I’ve worked on projects ranging from forecasting shorebird populations and analyzing vessel compliance in the Santa Barbara Channel to building mapping tools for kelp restoration research. I’m particularly interested in combining data science, remote sensing, and field-based research to support conservation and sustainable resource management.
